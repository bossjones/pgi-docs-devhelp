class GUPnP.ControlError

GLib.Error codes used for errors in the #GUPNP_CONTROL_ERROR domain, during invocation of service actions.

The action name was invalid.

INVALID_ARGS = 402

The action arguments were invalid.

Out of sync (deprecated).

The action failed.
