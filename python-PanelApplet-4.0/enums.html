class PanelApplet.AppletOrient

Bases: GObject.GEnum

Type defining the orientation of the applet. The values may seem backward (e.g. PanelApplet.AppletOrient.RIGHT means the panel is on the left side), but this represents the direction the applet is oriented to.

UP = 0

The PanelApplet.Applet is on a horizontal panel, at the bottom of the screen. It is oriented towards the top of the screen.

DOWN = 1

The PanelApplet.Applet is on a horizontal panel, at the top of the screen. It is oriented towards the bottom of the screen.

LEFT = 2

The PanelApplet.Applet is on a vertical panel, at the right of the screen. It is oriented towards the left of the screen.

RIGHT = 3

The PanelApplet.Applet is on a vertical panel, at the left of the screen. It is oriented towards the right of the screen.
